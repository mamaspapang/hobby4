Installing Ombre Style Crochet Braids

Now that you're interested in trying out ombre style crochet braids, let's talk about the installation process. Here are the steps you need to follow to achieve a fabulous look:

1. Prepare Your Hair

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your natural hair. It's important to have a clean and moisturized base before installing the crochet braids.
  2. Detangle your hair and make sure it's completely free of knots and tangles.
  3. If desired, you can also trim your hair to ensure a smooth and even application.

2. Cornrow Preparation

  1. Cornrow your natural hair in the desired pattern. This will act as the foundation for attaching the crochet braids.
  2. Make sure the cornrows are neat and evenly spaced to provide a strong base for the braids.

3. Crochet Braid Installation

  1. Choose the ombre style crochet braids you prefer. You can opt for pre-looped crochet hair or individual braids for a more customized look.
  2. Starting from the back, attach the crochet braids to your cornrows using a crochet needle. Make sure they are securely attached to prevent them from coming loose.
  3. Continue attaching the crochet braids until you've achieved your desired style and coverage.
