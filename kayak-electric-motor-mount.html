Q: How does an electric motor mount work?

Q: Can I still paddle my kayak with an electric motor mount?

A: Absolutely! The beauty of an electric motor mount is its versatility. You can easily detach the motor and paddle the kayak manually whenever desired. This flexibility allows you to conserve battery power and engage in active paddling whenever you prefer.
