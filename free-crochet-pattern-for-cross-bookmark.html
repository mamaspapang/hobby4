Getting Started with Crochet Cross Bookmark

Before we begin, let me assure you that this crochet pattern is beginner-friendly. You don't need to be an expert to create this lovely bookmark. Just gather your crochet hooks, some yarn, and let's get started!

Materials You'll Need

  1. One skein of your favorite yarn in any color you like.
  2. A crochet hook suitable for the yarn weight you've chosen. I recommend using a 3.5mm or 4mm hook.
  3. A pair of scissors.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that we have everything ready, let's jump into the step-by-step instructions for creating this beautiful crochet cross bookmark:

  1. Start by creating a slipknot on your crochet hook.
  2. Chain 10 stitches.
  3. In the second chain from the hook, create a single crochet stitch.
  4. Single crochet in each of the next 8 stitches.
  5. Chain 1 and turn your work.
  6. Repeat the previous step for the next 4 rows.
  7. Chain 5 and turn your work.
  8. In the second chain from the hook, create a double crochet stitch.
  9. Double crochet in each of the next 3 stitches.
  10. Chain 1 and turn your work.
  11. Repeat the previous step for the next 4 rows.
  12. Chain 1 and turn your work.
  13. Single crochet in each of the next 8 stitches.
  14. Chain 1 and turn your work.
  15. Repeat the previous step for the next 4 rows.
  16. Fasten off and weave in any loose ends.

And voila! You've successfully created your very own crochet cross bookmark. Now you can use it to mark your favorite pages in your books or gift it to someone special. The possibilities are endless!
