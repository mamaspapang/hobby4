Creating the Aggie Basket

To create the Aggie Basket, you'll need a few materials. Get your hands on some colorful yarn, a crochet hook, and a yarn needle. Once you have everything ready, you can start crocheting following the steps below:

Step 1: Foundation Chain

Begin by making a foundation chain of the desired length. This will determine the size of your Aggie Basket. Make sure to crochet the chain loosely to ensure flexibility.

Step 2: Single Crochet Rows

Once you have your foundation chain, start single crocheting in rows. Follow the pattern instructions provided by Meghan Makes Do to create the intricate stitch pattern that makes the Aggie Basket unique.

Step 3: Shaping the Basket

Continue crocheting until you reach the desired height for your Aggie Basket. Once you're satisfied with the size, it's time to shape the basket. Follow the pattern instructions to make the handles and finish off your creation.
