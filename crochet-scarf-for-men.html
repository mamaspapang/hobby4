Materials Needed

Before getting started, let's talk about the materials you'll need for this project:

1. Yarn

You'll need two skeins of gray yarn. Make sure to choose a soft and warm yarn that will feel cozy against your skin.

2. Crochet Hook

Use a size H/8 or 5mm crochet hook to create the perfect tension for this scarf.

3. Scissors

You'll need a good pair of scissors to cut the yarn and tidy up your finished project.

4. Yarn Needle

A yarn needle will come in handy for weaving in any loose ends.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that we have all the necessary materials, let's dive into the step-by-step instructions for creating this beautiful scarf:

  1. Start by making a slip knot with your yarn and attaching it to the crochet hook.
  2. Create a foundation chain of 35 stitches. This will be the width of your scarf.
  3. In the third chain from the hook, double crochet. Double crochet in each chain across.
  4. Chain 2 and turn your work. Double crochet in the second stitch from the hook and each stitch across.
  5. Repeat step 4 until your scarf reaches the desired length. Make sure to end with a double crochet in the last stitch.
  6. Fasten off and weave in any loose ends with your yarn needle.

And just like that, you'll have a beautiful Wilton gray men scarf! The pattern is simple and repetitive, making it easy to memorize and work on while watching your favorite show or listening to some music.
