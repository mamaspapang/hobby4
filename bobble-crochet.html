Creating the Luxe Bobbles Crochet Throw Blanket

To create this beautiful throw blanket, you'll need a few supplies. First and foremost, you'll need a super bulky yarn in the color of your choice. The pattern recommends using Lion Brand Wool-Ease Thick & Quick, but feel free to use any brand that offers a similar weight and thickness. You'll also need a size M/N (9.0 mm) crochet hook to achieve the recommended gauge.

Instructions:

  1. Start by chaining 94 stitches.
  2. Row 1: Single crochet into the second chain from the hook, and continue to single crochet across the row. Turn your work.
  3. Row 2: Chain 1, single crochet into the first stitch, *chain 2, skip the next 2 stitches, and make a bobble stitch into the next stitch. Repeat from * until you have two stitches left. Chain 2, skip the next stitch, single crochet into the last stitch. Turn your work.
  4. Continue to follow the pattern, alternating between rows of single crochets and rows of bobbles, until your blanket reaches the desired length. Feel free to customize the size by adjusting the number of stitches and rows.
  5. Finish off your blanket by neatly weaving in any loose ends of yarn.

As you work through the Luxe Bobbles Crochet Throw Blanket pattern, you'll notice that the bobbles add a delightful texture to the finished piece. This texture not only looks great but also adds warmth by trapping air between the stitches. The bobble stitches are created by working several double crochet stitches into the same stitch and then pulling a loop through all the loops on the hook. This technique produces a raised stitch that stands out from the rest of the fabric.
