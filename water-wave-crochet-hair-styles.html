How to Install Freetress Water Wave Crochet Braids?

To install Freetress water wave crochet braids, you will need the following:

1. Freetress Water Wave Hair:

Purchase Freetress water wave hair in the desired color and length. This hair is available in various shades, so choose the one that matches your natural hair color or the color you desire.

2. Crochet Needle:

Get a crochet needle, which is a special tool used to install crochet braids. It makes the installation process quick and easy.

3. Leave-Out Hair:

If you prefer a more natural look, you can leave out some of your own hair around the hairline and parting area. Make sure to moisturize and protect the leave-out hair to prevent breakage.

4. Braiding Pattern:

Create a braiding pattern on your natural hair that will serve as the base for attaching the crochet braids. You can choose a simple cornrow pattern or any other pattern you like.

Once you have all the necessary supplies, follow these steps to install the Freetress water wave crochet braids:

  1. Start by braiding your natural hair into the desired pattern. This will provide a foundation for the crochet braids.
  2. Take a small section of the Freetress water wave hair and insert the crochet needle through the braid. Hook the hair onto the needle and pull it through the braid, creating a loop.
  3. Hold the ends of the hair and pull it through the loop, securing it in place.
  4. Continue this process, working your way around your head until all of your natural hair is covered with the crochet braids.
  5. Once all the braids are installed, you can style the crochet braids as desired. You can curl them, braid them, or leave them as they are for a natural look.

After installing the Freetress water wave crochet braids, you'll be amazed at how beautiful and versatile your hair looks. These braids are a great choice for any occasion and can last for several weeks with proper care.
