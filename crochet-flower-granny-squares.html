Step-by-Step Instructions

Creating a crochet flower granny square is easier than you might think. Follow these simple steps and you'll have a beautiful square in no time:

  1. Start by gathering the materials needed, including a crochet hook, yarn in your chosen colors, and a pair of scissors.
  2. Begin with the center of the flower by creating a magic circle. This will form the base for your granny square.
  3. Next, crochet a chain of stitches around the magic circle. This creates the petals of the flower.
  4. Continue crocheting additional rounds, adding more petals as desired. You can experiment with different stitches and colors to create a unique design.
  5. Once your flower granny square reaches the desired size, finish off by weaving in any loose ends and trimming excess yarn.
