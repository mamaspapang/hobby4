Materials Needed

  1. Yarn of your choice (preferably a medium weight yarn)
  2. Crochet hook (size recommended on the yarn label)
  3. A pair of scissors
  4. A tapestry needle

Instructions

  1. Start by chaining the required number of stitches, ensuring that the chain is long enough to fit comfortably around your leg.
  2. Join the chain in a circle using a slip stitch, making sure not to twist the chain.
  3. Create the desired length of the leg warmer by working rounds of double crochet stitches. Feel free to customize the length as per your preference.
  4. Once you've reached the desired length, it's time to add the fringe. Cut several strands of yarn, twice the desired length of the fringe. Fold the strands in half and attach them to the cuffs of the leg warmers using a crochet hook.
  5. Weave in any loose ends and trim the fringe to your desired length.
  6. Repeat the above steps to create the second leg warmer.
