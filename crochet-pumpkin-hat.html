Materials and Instructions

To make your crochet pumpkin hat, you'll need the following materials:

  1. Orange yarn (worsted weight)
  2. Green yarn (worsted weight)
  3. Crochet hook (size G)
  4. Yarn needle
  5. Scissors

Step 1: Crocheting the Hat

Begin by crocheting the base of the hat using the orange yarn. Start with a magic ring and work single crochet stitches in a round until you achieve the desired circumference. It's essential to measure the head size for which you're making the hat to ensure a proper fit. Feel free to add more rounds if needed, and don't forget to join each round with a slip stitch.

Step 2: Creating the Pumpkin Design

Once the hat base is complete, it's time to add the pumpkin design. Switch to the green yarn and crochet a row of chain stitches, leaving a long tail for sewing later. Attach the chain to the hat, forming a circle, and secure it with single crochet stitches around the circumference. This will be the stem of the pumpkin.

Next, switch back to the orange yarn and create small crochet circles or oval shapes to resemble pumpkin sections. Join each section to the hat by sewing it securely with the yarn needle. Be creative with your placement, alternating the sizes and positions of the pumpkin sections to achieve a realistic look.
