What You'll Need

  1. Crochet hook (size G)
  2. Yarn in various colors (red, black, and white)
  3. Eyes for the plushie
  4. Polyester stuffing
  5. Yarn needle
  6. Scissors

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Start by crocheting the body of the Red Panda using red yarn. Follow the pattern instructions carefully to create the correct shape and size.
  2. Next, crochet the head, ears, and paws separately using black yarn. Don't forget to stuff them with polyester filling to give them a three-dimensional look.
  3. Attach the head to the body using a yarn needle and secure it with tight stitches.
  4. Sew on the ears and paws, making sure they are evenly spaced for a balanced look.
  5. Finally, add the eyes using either safety eyes or by embroidering them with black yarn.
  6. Voila! Your Red Panda amigurumi is complete.
