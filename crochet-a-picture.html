How to Crochet the Ava Dishcloth

Materials Needed

  1. Medium-weight cotton yarn in your preferred color
  2. 5.5mm crochet hook
  3. Scissors

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Chain 34 stitches to start your foundation row.
  2. Double crochet in the 4th chain from the hook.
  3. Chain 1, skip the next stitch, and double crochet in the next stitch.
  4. Repeat this V-stitch pattern until the last stitch, and double crochet in the last stitch.
  5. Chain 2 and turn your work.
  6. Double crochet in the chain-1 space of the V-stitch from the previous row.
  7. Continue the V-stitch pattern until the end of the row.
  8. Continue repeating steps 5-7 until your dishcloth reaches the desired size.
  9. Fasten off and weave in any loose ends.
