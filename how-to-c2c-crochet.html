What is keyword ?

At its core, keyword is all about creating beautiful crochet blankets using the C2C (corner-to-corner) technique. It's a unique crochet method that allows you to work diagonally from one corner of the blanket to the other. This technique creates a stunning visual effect that resembles a graph or a tiled pattern. It's a popular choice among crocheters because of its versatility and the endless possibilities it offers.

How does the C2C technique work?

The C2C technique involves crocheting small squares, or tiles, using different colors of yarn. These squares are then joined together to create the overall design of the blanket. The beauty of the C2C technique lies in its simplicity and adaptability. You can create intricate patterns or simple designs, depending on your skill level and preferences. It's like painting by numbers, but with yarn!

One of the major benefits of using the C2C technique is that it allows you to easily customize the size of your blanket. Since you're working from one corner to the other, you can simply add or remove squares to adjust the dimensions. This flexibility makes the C2C technique perfect for blankets of all sizes, from baby blankets to large throws.
