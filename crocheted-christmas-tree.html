Materials Needed:

Before we begin, gather the following materials:

  • Yarn in various shades of green
  • Crochet hooks in sizes E (3.5mm) and G (4.0mm)
  • Scissors
  • Yarn needle (for weaving in ends)
  • Stitch markers (optional)

Pattern Instructions:

Step 1: Crocheting the Base

  1. To start, make a magic ring and work six single crochets (sc) into the ring. Pull the ring tight, and then slip stitch (sl st) into the first sc to join the round.
  2. Continue working in a round, increasing in each stitch. Round 2: Work two sc in each stitch around. Round 3: *Sc in the next stitch, 2 sc in the following stitch* - repeat from * to * around.
  3. Repeat Round 3 until the base has reached your desired size, making sure to keep count of your stitches. This will give your tree stability and shape.

Step 2: Crocheting the Tree

  1. Change to a darker shade of green yarn.
  2. Work one round of single crochets, evenly spacing them around the base. This helps create a smooth transition from the base to the tree.
  3. To create the tree shape, we'll be working in rows. Start with a chain (ch) and turn your work.
  4. Continue working in rows, gradually decreasing the number of stitches in each row until you reach the top of the tree.

Keep following the free crochet pattern, adjusting the height and width according to your preference. You can also experiment with different color combinations to make your mini tree uniquely yours.
