Step 2: V-Stitch Rows

Once you have your foundation chain, it's time to start working on the V-Stitch pattern. Here's how:

  1. Row 1: Double crochet in the 4th chain from the hook. Ch 1, skip the next chain, and double crochet in the next chain. Repeat this pattern until the end of the row, ending with a double crochet in the last chain.
  2. Row 2: Ch 3 (counts as a double crochet), turn your work. Skip the first stitch, then work a V-Stitch (double crochet, ch 1, double crochet) in each ch 1 space across the row. Finish the row with a double crochet in the turning chain from the previous row.
  3. Rows 3 and beyond: Repeat Row 2 until your shawl reaches the desired size. Feel free to switch colors or create stripes to add some variation to your shawl.

Continue working on your V-Stitch Triangle Shawl until it reaches your desired size.
