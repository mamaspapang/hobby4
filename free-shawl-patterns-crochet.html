Materials Needed:

To create the Rivers Shawl, you will need the following materials:

  1. Crochet Hook: The pattern recommends using a Size G/6 (4 mm) crochet hook.
  2. Yarn: The pattern suggests using Light (3) weight yarn. Approximately 600-700 yards of yarn will be needed.
  3. Scissors
  4. Tapestry Needle

Instructions:

Step 1: Foundation Chain

To start the shawl, create a foundation chain of 75 stitches. The pattern provides detailed instructions on how to make the foundation chain.

Step 2: Main Body

Once you have completed the foundation chain, you will move on to the main body of the shawl. This section of the pattern includes various stitches and stitch combinations to create the beautiful texture of the shawl. The pattern provides row-by-row instructions, making it easy to follow along.

Step 3: Edging

After completing the main body of the shawl, you will move on to the edging. The edging adds a finished look to the shawl and complements the overall design. The pattern provides instructions on how to create the edging, including the specific stitches and stitch counts.

Once you have finished crocheting the Rivers Shawl, you can block it to enhance the drape and shape of the shawl. Blocking is a process where you wet the shawl and shape it to the desired dimensions. The pattern includes tips on how to block the shawl for the best results.
