Creating the Crocheted Cross Bookmarks

First, start by selecting your scrap yarn in various colors. This is a great way to use up any leftover yarn from previous projects. You can choose a single color for a simple and elegant bookmark or mix and match different colors for a more vibrant design. The choice is yours!

Step 1: Making the Base

To begin, make a magic ring and work a chain of 3 stitches. Then, double crochet 15 stitches into the magic ring. This will create the base of the cross bookmark. Once you have completed the round, slip stitch into the first stitch to join the round.

Step 2: Adding the Crossbeam

Next, chain 3 to create the height of the crossbeam and double crochet into the next stitch. Repeat this process for the next 4 stitches. This will create the crossbeam of the bookmark. Once you have completed the crossbeam, slip stitch into the first stitch to join the round.

Step 3: Finishing Touches

To finish the crocheted cross bookmark, fasten off the yarn and weave in any loose ends. You can also add a tassel or ribbon to the top of the bookmark for an extra decorative touch. Once you have completed these steps, your crocheted cross bookmark is ready to be used or gifted!
