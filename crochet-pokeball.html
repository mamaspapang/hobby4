Let's dive into the details of this pattern:

Materials You'll Need:

  1. Red and white yarn
  2. A crochet hook
  3. Stuffing material

The Crochet Pattern:

1. Start by crocheting a magic ring with the red yarn.

2. Work 6 single crochet stitches into the magic ring.

3. Pull the magic ring tight to close the circle.

4. Continue crocheting in the round, working 2 single crochet stitches into each stitch (12 stitches).

5. Work 1 single crochet stitch into each stitch (12 stitches).

6. Change to white yarn and work 1 single crochet stitch into each stitch (12 stitches).

7. Continue crocheting in the round, alternating between red and white yarn every 2 rounds, until you have 6 red and 6 white rounds.

8. Stuff the Pokeball with the stuffing material.

9. Decrease 1 stitch in each alternating round until you have 6 stitches left.

10. Cut the yarn, leaving a long tail. Thread the tail through the remaining stitches, pull tight to close the opening, and fasten off.
