How to Make a Crochet Mini Purse

To start making this crochet mini purse, you'll need a few basic materials. First, make sure you have the right yarn and crochet hook. You can choose any color you like to make it more personal. Then, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by creating a foundation chain of the desired length for the purse strap. This will depend on how long you want it to be when worn.
  2. Next, crochet a few rows in single crochet stitch to create the strap. Adjust the number of rows depending on the desired width of the strap.
  3. Once you're satisfied with the length and width of the strap, it's time to make the purse itself. Fold the strap in half and join the ends with slip stitches to create a loop.
  4. Continue crocheting in rounds, working on both sides of the strap to create the body of the purse. You can crochet as many rounds as you want, depending on how deep you want the purse to be.
  5. Finish off by crocheting a round of single crochet stitches around the top edge of the purse to give it a neat finishing touch. Fasten off and weave in any loose ends.

And voila! You've just created your very own crochet mini purse. It's such a fun and simple project, perfect for beginners and experienced crocheters alike.
