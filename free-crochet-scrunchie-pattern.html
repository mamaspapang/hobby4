Getting Started

Before we get into the details, here's what you'll need:

  • Crochet hook (size size)
  • Cotton yarn (color)
  • Elastic hairband
  • Scissors
  • Embroidery needle

Step 1: Creating the Base

Start by creating a slip knot with the yarn and hook. Then, insert the hook into the elastic hairband and pull the yarn through the loop. This will create a chain of stitches around the hairband. Continue until you reach the desired length for your scrunchie.

Step 2: Adding the Crochet Stitches

Once you have the base ready, it's time to add some crochet stitches. This is where you can get creative and choose your favorite stitch pattern. You can try the single crochet, double crochet, or even a combination of different stitches to achieve various textures and designs.

Begin by making a foundation row of stitches around the base. Then, continue with the chosen stitch pattern for the next few rows. Remember to maintain a consistent tension throughout to ensure a neat and even finish.
