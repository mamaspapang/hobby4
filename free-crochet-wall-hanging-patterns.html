Q: How can I hang a crochet wall hanging?

A: Hanging a crochet wall hanging is easy! You can use a wooden dowel or a curtain rod as the base for your crochet piece. Simply attach the crochet wall hanging to the dowel or rod using yarn or decorative ribbon, and then hang it on the wall using nails, adhesive hooks, or a mounting bracket.

Q: Can I wash a crochet wall hanging?

A: Most crochet wall hangings can be gently hand-washed using mild soap and lukewarm water. However, it's best to check the care instructions provided with the specific piece or consult the maker for guidance to ensure the longevity of your crochet wall hanging.
