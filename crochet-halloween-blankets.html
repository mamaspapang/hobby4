Creating the Halloween Blanket

To create this Halloween Blanket, you will need the following materials:

  • Worsted weight yarn in various Halloween colors such as orange, black, and purple
  • A crochet hook size H
  • A pair of scissors

Step 1: Starting Chain

To begin, make a slip knot with your yarn and then chain 100 stitches. This will serve as the foundation for your blanket.

Step 2: Single Crochet

Once you have your starting chain, work a single crochet stitch into the second chain from your hook. Continue to single crochet all the way across the chain. This will create your first row of stitches.

Step 3: Repeat Rows

Continue to repeat the single crochet stitch in each row until your blanket reaches the desired length. You can choose to make a small lap blanket or a larger full-size blanket. The choice is yours!
